Online Tutoring Philosophy

The primary purpose of this online tutoring program is to assist students who are either struggling or wish to excel in their courses. Success in 1st/2nd year courses is crucial for getting good co-op jobs and making an impression on professors whom you wish to do research with.

Online tutoring makes it easy for students to gain extra assistance without commiting any extra time to meet at some location.

What do you get?

Private/Group Sessions

Meetings occur either weekly or biweekly and can be solo or in a group. Online zoom meetings allow for multiple people to meet while all at different places.

Custom Study Plans

To help keep you on track, study plans will be created with hard deadlines based on recommended questions in course textbooks.

Questions From Past Exams

Having taken these courses in the past, I have access to many past midterm exams; I will provide these questions to all clients when preparing for exams.
