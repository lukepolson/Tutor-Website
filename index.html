Purpose

The goal of this online tutoring program is to assist students who are struggling and/or want extra practice in their courses. Success in university courses will help you obtain co-op jobs and research positions.

Online tutoring makes it easy for students to obtain assistance without commiting to any travel time.

Why Us?

Experience

Every tutor here is a recent graduate of UVIC and knows the key subject material in each course. We also know what style of questions are typically asked on tests.

Questions From Past Exams

Since we took the courses a few years ago, we have access to many past exams and practice exam problems.

Private/Group Sessions

Meeting times are negotiated with clients. Sessions are either 1 on 1 or you can bring a group of peers for a discounted price!

Pricing

Get discounted pricing if you connect with a friend or a group! For specific details, please contact us.
